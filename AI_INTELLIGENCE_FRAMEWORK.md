# SUUKA CLEAN AI INTELLIGENCE & ADMIN CONTROL FRAMEWORK

## AI GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLE
AI SHALL NEVER:

- Approve or reject cleaners
- Suspend accounts
- Terminate users
- Process refunds automatically
- Assign penalties
- Approve payments
- Make inventory purchases
- Allocate cleaners without approval
- Modify system settings
- Delete records

AI MAY:

- Analyze data
- Detect patterns
- Generate recommendations
- Flag anomalies
- Predict trends
- Suggest actions
- Generate reports

Final decisions must always be made by authorized personnel.
